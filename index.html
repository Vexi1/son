Featured

Mousepad recommendations

Slower

Slower Pads

Slower (control) mousepads are more suited for Tactical FPS games. They minimize shaky aim due to high static friction so you can hold angles, and allow for fast stops after flicks due to higher dynamic friction.

Balanced

Balanced Pads

Balanced pads are preferred by most people. They can be used for any game as they allow for some control and stopping power without lacking speed and a smooth glide.

Speed

Speed Pads

Faster (speed) pads are well suited for tracking heavy games. They try to reduce friction as much as possible allowing for a very smooth consistent glide and very minimal stopping power.
